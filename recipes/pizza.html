Easy Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Here is a quick and easy pepperoni pizza recipe that you can make!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fast rise yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups pepporoni slices
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup shredded montery jack cheese
  • 3 tablesppons grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In large bowl, mix first 4 ingredients
  2. Mix water and oil; add to flour mixture
  3. Turn onto floured surface; knead for 2 minutes
  4. Place in a greased bowl; turning to grease top
  5. Cover and let rise for 20 minutes
  6. Punch down; place on 12in, greased pizza pan
  7. Pat into a circle
  8. Topping: Mix tomato sauce, italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper and spread over
  9. Put a few pepperoni slices on top of sauce
  10. Sprinkle with 1/2 the mozzarella; 1/2 the montery jack, and 1/2 the parmesan
  11. Put the rest of the pepporoni on
  12. repeat the cheese layer
  13. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until light brown
